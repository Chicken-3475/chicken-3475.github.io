Aircraft Production Timeline

This aircraft was ordered new by CIT Aerospace International - ?

Rolled off Airbus production line at Toulouse, France - September 2010

Powered by General Electric GE CF6-80E1A3 engines

First flown as 'F-WWKS' in primer with the standard Qantas tail - November 19, 2010

Entered onto Australian Aircraft Register as VH-EBP - November 19, 2010

Registered to C.I.T. Leasing Corporation, New York

Leased to Qantas Airways Ltd, Sydney as the registered operator

Accepted by CIT Leasing / Qantas at Toulouse, France - December 13, 2010

Departed Toulouse on the delivery flight to Australia as QF6020 - December 14, 2010

It arrived at Sydney (Kingsford Smith) on completion of the delivery flight - December 16, 2010

Delivery route: Toulouse - Singapore - Sydney

During pre-service maintenance the aircraft was named 'Ningaloo Reef'

Operated its first revenue service Sydney (Kingsford Smith) - Perth as QF581 - December 19, 2010

Registered to Wells Fargo Bank Northwest National Association, Salt Lake City, Utah - November 11, 2016

Leased to Qantas Airways, Sydney as the registered operator

Registered to Sapphire Leasing (AOE 4) Limited, Shannon, Ireland - August 29, 2018

Leased to Qantas Airways Limited, Sydney as the registered operator

Ferried Sydney (Kingsford Smith) - Melbourne (Avalon) as QF6115 - April 03, 2020

Withdrawn from service and stored at Melbourne (Avalon) due to the COVID-19 pandemic
